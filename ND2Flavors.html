Our Fabulous Flavors!

At Swirl’s Frozen Yogurt, we offer a rotating selection of delicious flavors, from classic favorites like vanilla and chocolate to exciting seasonal specials. Whether you crave something fruity, creamy, or a little adventurous, there’s always something new to swirl!

Here are our monthly specials - come grab a taste while you can!

Pumpkin Spice Swirl Celebrate the season with creamy pumpkin yogurt, spiced with cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove. It’s fall in a cup—perfect for cozy sweater weather!
Apple Pie Delight A delicious blend of sweet apples, cinnamon, and buttery pie crust flavors. his yogurt will give you all the warm, nostalgic vibes of a fresh-baked apple pie!
Maple Pecan Crunch Rich maple flavor combined with the nutty crunch of pecans for a smooth and satisfying fall treat. It’s the perfect balance of sweet and savory!

Here are some highlights of our current flavors!

Classic Vanilla Smooth, creamy, and timeless. Our Vanilla Classic is the perfect balance of sweetness, making it the ideal base for any topping or a delightful treat all on its own!
Classic Chocolate Indulgent and velvety, this chocolate frozen yogurt satisfies every craving with its deep, rich flavor. Perfect for chocolate lovers looking for a guilt-free indulgence!
Berry Blast A fruity explosion of strawberry, raspberry, and blueberry blended into a tangy-sweet swirl. Perfect for berry lovers looking for a refreshing treat!
Caramel Pretzel Delight Sweet meets salty in this addictive combo of caramel yogurt with crunchy pretzel pieces. It’s a flavor adventure you won’t want to miss!
Tropical Twist Transport yourself to paradise with this blend of mango, pineapple, and coconut. A smooth, tropical escape in every bite!
